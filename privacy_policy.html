Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Silence Timer: Sleep & Focus (the "App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, location data, or any other personal data. All app data and settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The App requires certain permissions to function correctly. These are used strictly for the app's features and not for any other purpose:

  • Notification Policy Access: Needed to mute and restore your phone's sound categories (Ringer, Notifications, etc.) as part of the timer feature.
  • Post Notifications: Used to display the active timer countdown and notify you when the timer completes.
  • Run at Startup: Used to restore any active timers if your device is restarted.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud services. Your data remains entirely on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information, making it safe for all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our GitHub repository.
